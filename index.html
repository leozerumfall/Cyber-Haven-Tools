How it works

From listing to delivery

01

List or browse

Sellers submit a listing with a description and a hash of the file. Buyers browse by category.

02

Review & approve

Every listing is checked against platform policy before it goes live. No exceptions.

03

Pay on‑chain

Buyer sends BTC or ETH to a unique address generated for that order. No cards, no PII required.

04

Confirm & deliver

Once the transaction confirms, the file unlocks automatically and the seller gets paid out.
